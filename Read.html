With the lady I know

under a moonlit sky
she would come, dressed in blue
and see beyond my facade
to the unfathomable despair in my eyes,
she would smile.
she would sit in tranquil ,
till the faint clouds passes by the moon,
A lady ,drenched in moonglow
in front of my smoky eyes!
Not a fairy,she would be.
Not an illusion to hallucinate.
A crystal reflection indeed
but the mirror, an entangled web
of 99 endeavours.
-Rituporna

Cape Jasmine

You painted love in the whites
Of cape Jasmine,
To make it allure me more,
With its casting white amid the wild green foliage.
Till dusk of today,
it only reminded me of the lively spring ,
Taking me back from autumn or winter!
You added to its fragrance, the fragrance
of all the love you hold.
In a dream I would dream ,
Of you creating a symphony,
Manifesting everything beautiful we find ,
or everything that made
our love look more beautiful.
The dawn no more looks
dreadful to throw me in seclusion,
And the dusk no more fading away ;
For , I peep through the window
thecape Jasmine
Blooming out there,
Narrating everything you whispered to it today.
-Rituporna

Melody

brought the call of the crimson sky
and the shimmering water of the stream.
The green lea already captured me
from every corner of my sight.
That muddy path greeted ,
with unknown yet promising gestures.
Eventide has always been the best for me,
they know.
In that hour of departure of the day,
I breathe out despair or gulp in hope;
hope to live.

Towards the Horizon

We are all valiant.
aren't we?
we have been sailing this far ,
with the horizon in front of us,
farthest it is ,
yet, the voyage admires it,
in the wave of every minute
drifting through the unravelling,
we all have been sailing and have to.
this voyage of life in the sea of manoeuvres
we are obliged to sail and sail
the sea will be ruthless of all
under a dreadly sky with roaring clouds,
the heavy drops will drench us whole
and sink us down.
the same wide stretch of blue hue ,
with the gushing wind and the Sun rays washing
through us, ain't that be ecstatic?
Look, the horizon awaits us
let's move with the unknown hours,
for we are sailors in this vastness full of mysteries.
-Rituporna
